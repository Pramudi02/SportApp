# CourtFinder - User Guide

## Getting Started

### First Launch
1. **Registration**: Open the app and tap "Don't have an account? Register"
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm Password
3. Tap "Register" to create your account

### Login
If you already have an account:
1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap the eye icon to show/hide your password
3. Tap "Login"

## Troubleshooting

### No Locations Showing
- Pull down to refresh the list
- Check your internet connection
- The app will use dummy data if API is unavailable

### Favorites Not Saving
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check device storage permissions
- Try restarting the app

### App Crashes or Freezes
- Force close and reopen the app
- Clear app cache if needed
- Ensure you have the latest version

### Login Issues
- Verify email format is correct
- Password must be at least 6 characters
- Try registering a new account if issues persist
